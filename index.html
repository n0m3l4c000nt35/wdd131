Buenos Aires, Argentina

Buenos Aires, the capital of Argentina, is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city located in the central region of the country, along the shores of the Río de la Plata. With over 3 million inhabitants, it is the political, cultural, and economic hub of Argentina. Buenos Aires is known for its European architecture, rich cultural life, and tango. The city offers a wide range of attractions such as the iconic Obelisk, the Teatro Colón, the historic Plaza de Mayo, and the neighborhoods of San Telmo, Palermo, and La Boca. It is also famous for its cuisine, especially its meats and the classic Argentine asado.
